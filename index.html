INTRODUCTION

Designing and developing a website in 3-7 days is incredibly and purely insane unless you are building static pages for a blueprint website or a temporary website.

Web Design is an art just like graphic design and with that being mentioned, designing requires ample time to come up with a carefully curated and crafted, full-blown webpages that will put your business at the frontline this year, 2019. I usually breakdown a website into phases (functionalities are usually factored in) and assign activities to these phases with allotted time for each activity just before I get down to the actual Development.

The Design Phase:

This phase entails summing up the entire project work flow into some kind of a lifecycle (if you are familiar with SDLC, Software Development Lifecycle); Activities undertaken at this phase include:

1. Functional Requirements Collection- Specific Entities to be included on your webpages and all business logic summed up by client.

2. Building a prototype /A Blueprint (a mock website)- A static set of all required pages to simulate what the actual pages and functionalities should :
 work
 Look-and-feel-Aesthetics, colours and elements,
 Responsiveness (does the website adapt to Mobile, Laptops and desktop devices screen resolutions)
 Cross-browser compatibility
 I usually provide copies of blueprints for your evaluation and revision before we get down to building the real life product.

The Development Phase


This Phase is normally tightly nested (involving repetitive activities-debugging, testing and so on) with the previous phase and are set of repeated activities with the aim of achieving all specifications mentioned in the Design Phase.

The Activities are as always follows:-
1. Actual Development (writing Code) takes place- this includes transform user requirements into Business logic and Data Structures.
 Testing the Website- Does it Meet the user requirements, is it in adherence to the prototype/Blueprint (mentioned in the Design Phase)?
 Are user data and all Business logic captured?
 Security Check- Is the website secure from attackers?2. Debugging- Entails hunting Down Bugs (program errors) that are causing the website to produce unexpected results. Activity is closely coupled with 1 & 2.


The Deployment (Hosting your website)

This is popularly known has Hosting- is the website ready for hosting/deployment? Choose a webhosting plan that suits your needs and budget, please be open to mention if you are going to host the website on your own. Hosting fees usually apply, kindly checkout with various web hosting companies.

Maintenance

This is a standalone phase that comes after the mentioned phases.Maintenance entails the following:-

Improving Aesthetics.
Maintaining SEO (Search Engine Optimization) through provision of relevant content to generate organic traffic (site visits via google search results).Does your site rank in top 5 Google search or Bing results when user queries certain words related to your business?.
